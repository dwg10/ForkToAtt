• In this study, you will make several decisions regarding baskets.
  • Each basket contains a combination of balls. The balls have one of three possible colours, and each colour represents a different value.
  • Your goal is to select the basket that is the most valuable for each round.
  • There is no physical basket, but your decisions will have real consequences. for your payment.
  • Two randomly selected participants will receive a fixed payment of €{{Constants.FixedPayment}}.
  • Additionally, you can obtain an bonus payment of €{{Constants.BonusPayment}} depending on your decisions.
