• In this study, you will make several decisions regarding baskets.
  • Each basket contains a combination of balls. The balls can have one of three possible colours, and each colour represents a different value.
  • Your goal is to select the most valuable basket in each round.
  • There is no physical basket, but your decisions can have real consequences for your payment.
  • Two randomly selected participants will receive a fixed payment of €{{ Constants.FixedPayment }}.
  • Additionally, you can obtain a bonus payment of €{{ Constants.BonusPayment }} depending on your decisions.
